About Ali R. Salman, MD

Fellowship-trained vitreoretinal surgeon with a focus on patient-centered care.

Meet Dr. Salman

Dr. Ali R. Salman is a board-certified ophthalmologist and vitreoretinal surgeon who provides medical and surgical care for complex retinal disease. His clinical scope includes medical retina, retinal laser procedures, intravitreal therapy, and vitreoretinal surgery.

He completed fellowship training in vitreoretinal surgery and focuses on macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, retinal tears and detachments, vascular retinal disease, and vitreoretinal interface disorders.

Dr. Salman is an invited peer reviewer for multiple retina and ophthalmology journals and maintains an active academic interest in surgical outcomes and retinal imaging research.

Dr. Salman also has a strong passion for humanitarian aid work and has worked with the nonprofit United2Heal for the past decade, helping send medical supplies to countries and regions in need around the world.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Salman enjoys spending time with his wife and two children, playing soccer, exploring the outdoors, and traveling.

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information may be handled when you use this informational website.

Current Website Status

This website is informational. Ali R. Salman, MD does not currently operate an independent clinic through this website, and this website is not currently accepting patients, scheduling appointments, or providing medical care.

Information You Choose to Provide

This website does not contain an embedded patient intake form. If you choose to contact Dr. Salman by email or through the linked secure inquiry service, you may provide information such as your name, email address, contact details, and message content. Please do not send urgent medical concerns or unnecessary sensitive health information by standard email.

Secure Inquiry Service

The secure inquiry button links to a third-party Paubox form. Information submitted through that form is handled through Paubox's service and may also be subject to Paubox's applicable privacy and security terms. Submitting information through that service does not create a physician-patient relationship and does not mean that Dr. Salman is accepting you as a patient.

Automatically Collected Technical Information

Like most websites, this site and its hosting or infrastructure providers may receive basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and access times. This information is used to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website. This site does not intentionally use advertising pixels or behavioral advertising scripts.

How Information May Be Used

Information you voluntarily provide may be used to review and respond to your inquiry, maintain records of communications, protect the website, and comply with applicable obligations. It is not used to schedule appointments or provide medical care through this website.

No Emergency or Medical Advice

This website is not monitored for emergencies. Do not use this website, email, or the secure inquiry link for urgent symptoms or emergency medical needs. If you believe you may have a medical emergency, call 911 or seek immediate in-person medical care.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the website's purpose, services, or data-handling practices change, including if a future clinic or patient-care workflow is established.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email info@dralisalman.com.
